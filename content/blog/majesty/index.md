# The Unparalleled Majesty of "The Lord of the Rings"

J.R.R. Tolkien's *legendarium* is a work of unparalleled depth and beauty. As the great **Archmage** of fantasy literature, Tolkien created a world that has captivated readers for generations.

The `Valar` shaped the world, and Tolkien shaped our imagination.

> "I am a hobbit in all but size."
>
> -- J.R.R. Tolkien

## Why It Endures

The Lord of the Rings endures because it is not merely a fantasy novel. It is a work of subcreation, a secondary world so complete and so internally consistent that it achieves a kind of literary reality.
