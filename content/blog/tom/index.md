# Why Tom Bombadil Was a Mistake

*An unpopular opinion, I know.*

The great **Archmage** of fantasy writing made very few mistakes, but Tom Bombadil was one of them.

## Who Is He?

Nobody knows. Not even Tolkien seemed to have a clear answer. Tom Bombadil is immune to the One Ring, older than the elves, and apparently more powerful than Sauron within his own domain.

## The Pacing Problem

You're reading The Fellowship of the Ring. The hobbits have just barely escaped the Shire, Black Riders are on their tail, tension is building, and then you spend several chapters with a merry fellow who speaks in rhyming couplets.
