Why Glorfindel is More Impressive than Legolas

You want to talk about impressive elves? Forget Legolas, let's talk about Glorfindel.

The Balrog Slayer

In the Fall of Gondolin, Glorfindel fought a Balrog in single combat to protect fleeing refugees. He didn't just fight it, he killed it. Yes, the same type of creature that took down Gandalf.

He died in the process, but was so impressive that the Valar sent him back to Middle-earth.

The Witch-king Fled from Him

When the Nazgûl were hunting Frodo, it was Glorfindel who rode out to meet them. The Witch-king himself refused to face Glorfindel directly.
